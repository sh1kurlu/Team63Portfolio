Hour Of Code

On November 7, 2023, Team 63 organized a transformative "Hour of Code" event at Zangilan (Karabakh) Secondary School No 24. The aim was to enhance the learning experience for students in an environment with limited technical support and resources, introducing them to the exciting world of coding and providing an engaging learning experience.
